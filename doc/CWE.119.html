CWE-119: Improper Restriction of Operations within the Bounds of a Memory Buffer [CWE.119]

RULES

Avoid accessing arrays out of bounds [CWE.119.ARRAY]
Avoid unvalidated input in array indexes [CWE.119.ARRAYSEC]
Do not use an integer outside the range of [0, 31] as the amount of a shift [CWE.119.BSA]
Prevent external processes from blocking on output or error streams [CWE.119.BUSSB]
Do not use resources that have been freed [CWE.119.FREE]
