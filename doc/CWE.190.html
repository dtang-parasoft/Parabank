CWE-190: Integer Overflow or Wraparound [CWE.190]

RULES

Do not use an integer outside the range of [0, 31] as the amount of a shift [CWE.190.BSA]
Avoid using compound assignment operators in cases which may cause overflow [CWE.190.CACO]
Do not cast primitive data types to lower precision [CWE.190.CLP]
Avoid calculations which result in overflow or NaN [CWE.190.ICO]
Avoid integer overflows [CWE.190.INTOVERF]
Use unsigned right shift instead of division when overflow is possible [CWE.190.IOF]
