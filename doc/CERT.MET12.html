MET12-J: Do not use finalizers [CERT.MET12]

RULES

Avoid empty "finalize()" methods [CERT.MET12.EF]
Call 'super.finalize()' from 'finalize()' [CERT.MET12.FCF]
Avoid redundant 'finalize()' methods which only call the superclass' 'finalize()' method [CERT.MET12.FCSF]
Do not use 'finalize()' methods to unregister listeners [CERT.MET12.FM]
Call 'super.finalize()' in the "finally" block of 'finalize()' methods [CERT.MET12.IFF]
Give "finalize()" methods "protected" access [CERT.MET12.MFP]
Do not define 'finalize()' method in bean classes [CERT.MET12.MNDF]
Do not call 'finalize()' explicitly [CERT.MET12.NCF]
Do not overload the 'finalize()' method [CERT.MET12.OF]
