API10-Insufficient Logging & Monitoring [OWASP2019.API10]

RULES

Do not call the 'printStackTrace()' method of "Throwable" objects [OWASP2019.API10.ACPST]
Avoid writing to Consoles [OWASP2019.API10.ACW]
Avoid using multiple loggers, use logging levels instead [OWASP2019.API10.AUML]
Do not log confidential or sensitive information [OWASP2019.API10.CONSEN]
Avoid debug levels which are too high in Tomcat's 'server.xml' [OWASP2019.API10.DLTH]
Ensure all sensitive method invocations are logged [OWASP2019.API10.ENFL]
Ensure all exceptions are either logged with a standard logger or rethrown [OWASP2019.API10.LGE]
Avoid logging sensitive Hibernate-related information at the 'info' level in 'log4j.properties' files [OWASP2019.API10.LHII]
Ensure that the logging level checked matches the level of the called logging method [OWASP2019.API10.LLM]
Avoid calling print methods of 'System.err' or 'System.out' [OWASP2019.API10.SIO]
Protect against log forging [OWASP2019.API10.TDLOG]
