Enterprise JavaBeans 3 [EJB.EJB3]

RULES

Avoid ignored or invalid annotations [EJB.EJB3.AIA]
Avoid IllegalStateException when using transactions [EJB.EJB3.AISE]
Do not start business method names with "ejb" [EJB.EJB3.BMN]
Improper signature of the annotated method in EJB bean [EJB.EJB3.CMF]
Call 'InvocationContext.proceed()' from @AroundInvoke methods [EJB.EJB3.CPIM]
Do not expose @AroundInvoke method in business interface [EJB.EJB3.EAII]
Avoid improper use of @Id annotation [EJB.EJB3.IDA]
Ensure @IdClass is Serializable and defines "equals()" and "hashCode()" methods [EJB.EJB3.IDCS]
Do not inject @Stateful beans into @Stateless beans [EJB.EJB3.ISB]
Do not extend other @MessageDriven beans from a @MessageDriven bean [EJB.EJB3.MDBS]
Ensure @MessageDriven beans specify a MessageListener [EJB.EJB3.MDML]
Do not declare multiple "@Timeout" methods [EJB.EJB3.MTM]
Always specify "unitName" with @PersistenceContext [EJB.EJB3.PCUN]
Avoid EJB 3 methods without security annotations [EJB.EJB3.PERMIT]
Do not declare an interface both @Local and @Remote [EJB.EJB3.RLI]
Specify a @Remove method for @Stateful beans [EJB.EJB3.RMSB]
Ensure instance variables of @Stateful beans are Serializable [EJB.EJB3.SIVS]
Ensure parameters and return type of remote business methods are Serializable [EJB.EJB3.SRBM]
Access beans through a local interface [EJB.EJB3.ULI]
