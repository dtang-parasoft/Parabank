OBJ05-J: Defensively copy private mutable class members before returning their references [CERT.OBJ05]

RULES

Enforce returning a defensive copy in 'clone()' methods [CERT.OBJ05.CPCL]
Do not pass user-given mutable objects directly to certain types [CERT.OBJ05.MPT]
