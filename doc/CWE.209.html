CWE-209: Generation of Error Message Containing Sensitive Information [CWE.209]

RULES

Do not call the 'printStackTrace()' method of "Throwable" objects [CWE.209.ACPST]
Do not pass exception messages into output in order to prevent the application from leaking sensitive information [CWE.209.PEO]
Prevent exposure of sensitive data [CWE.209.SENS]
Avoid calling print methods of 'System.err' or 'System.out' [CWE.209.SIO]
