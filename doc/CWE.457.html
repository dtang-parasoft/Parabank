CWE-457: Use of Uninitialized Variable [CWE.457]

RULES

Avoid use before explicit initialization [CWE.457.NOTEXPLINIT]
Avoid use of fields before initialization in constructors and static initializers [CWE.457.NOTINITCTOR]
Avoid NullPointerException [CWE.457.NP]
Avoid uninitialized reads of fields before or during constructor execution [CWE.457.UIRC]
