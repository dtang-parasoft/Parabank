CWE-502: Deserialization of Untrusted Data [CWE.502]

RULES

Avoid parsing untrusted data with XMLDecoder [CWE.502.AUXD]
Assign 'protected' accessibility to 'readResolve()' and 'writeReplace()' methods in serializable classes [CWE.502.MASP]
Ensure that all fields are assigned by the 'readObject()' method and written out by the 'writeObject()' method [CWE.502.RWAF]
Disable LDAP deserialization [CWE.502.SC]
Safely serialize sensitive data [CWE.502.SSSD]
Validate objects before deserialization [CWE.502.VOBD]
