CWE-833: Deadlock [CWE.833]

RULES

Do not cause deadlocks by calling a "synchronized" method from a "synchronized" method [CWE.833.CSFS]
Do not acquire locks in different order [CWE.833.ORDER]
Release Locks in a "finally" block [CWE.833.RLF]
Do not perform synchronization nor call semaphore methods on an Object's 'this' reference [CWE.833.STR]
Do not use blocking methods while holding a lock [CWE.833.TSHL]
Use 'wait()' and 'notifyAll()' instead of polling loops [CWE.833.UWNA]
