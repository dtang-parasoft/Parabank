FIO13-J: Do not log sensitive information outside a trusted boundary [CERT.FIO13]

RULES

Do not log confidential or sensitive information [CERT.FIO13.CONSEN]
Avoid logging sensitive Hibernate-related information at the 'info' level in 'log4j.properties' files [CERT.FIO13.LHII]
Do not pass exception messages into output in order to prevent the application from leaking sensitive information [CERT.FIO13.PEO]
Prevent exposure of sensitive data [CERT.FIO13.SENS]
