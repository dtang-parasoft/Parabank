2.5.4-F: Numeric overflows [VVSG.2_5_4_F]

RULES

Do not use an integer outside the range of [0, 31] as the amount of a shift [VVSG.2_5_4_F.BSA]
Do not cast primitive data types to lower precision [VVSG.2_5_4_F.CLP]
Avoid calculations which result in overflow or NaN [VVSG.2_5_4_F.ICO]
Avoid integer overflows [VVSG.2_5_4_F.INTOVERF]
Use unsigned right shift instead of division when overflow is possible [VVSG.2_5_4_F.IOF]
