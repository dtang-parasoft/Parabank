A4-Insecure Design [OWASP2021.A4]

RULES

Avoid duplicated forms in the 'validation.xml' [OWASP2021.A4.DFV]
Avoid DNS lookups for decision making [OWASP2021.A4.DNSL]
Ensure validators are enabled in the 'struts-config.xml' [OWASP2021.A4.EV]
Ensure method arguments are serializable [OWASP2021.A4.ONS]
Ensure sufficient protection against multiple failed authentication attempts [OWASP2021.A4.PBFA]
Avoid storing usernames and passwords in plain text in Castor 'jdo-conf.xml' files [OWASP2021.A4.PCCF]
Avoid storing sensitive data in plaintext in a cookie [OWASP2021.A4.PLC]
Ensure Plugins are added in the 'struts-config.xml' [OWASP2021.A4.PLUGIN]
Avoid using plain text passwords in Axis 'wsdd' files [OWASP2021.A4.PTPT]
Ensure that passwords are not stored as plaintext and are sufficiently long [OWASP2021.A4.PWDPROP]
Ensure that passwords are not stored as plaintext and are sufficiently long [OWASP2021.A4.PWDXML]
Do not store non-serializable objects as HttpSession attributes [OWASP2021.A4.SNSO]
Do not store untrusted data in HTTP session [OWASP2021.A4.TDSESSION]
Use the ''getSecure()'' and ''setSecure()'' methods to enforce the use of secure cookies [OWASP2021.A4.UOSC]
Avoid unencrypted passwords in WebSphere 'ibm-webservicesclient-ext.xmi' files [OWASP2021.A4.WCPWD]
Avoid unencrypted passwords in WebSphere 'ibm-webservices-ext.xmi' files [OWASP2021.A4.WPWD]
